Air Battery - A Free App to Monitor Battery Levels

September 02, 2024 — 09:00


My search for a good multi-device battery monitoring app ended today when I discovered Air Battery. In just a few minutes I had a widget installed in notification center with accurate battery readouts of my Macbook, iPhone, iPad and Apple Watch. I didn't have to install any client apps, just one on the Mac. 

Facts

  • Displays on Dock, Menu Bar or as a widget - or all three, you pick
  • Can be configured to show the battery levels of all Macs on your LAN
  • Homebrew installation
brew install lihaoyun6/tap/airbattery
  • Runs on macOS 11 or later
  • Hide any devices you do not want to see


Devices

  • Laptop
  • iPhone
  • iPad
  • Apple Watch
  • Air Pods
  • Magic Keyboard
  • Magic Mouse
  • Magic Trackpad


Air Battery is a free app and is distributed under a AGPL-3.0 license.
