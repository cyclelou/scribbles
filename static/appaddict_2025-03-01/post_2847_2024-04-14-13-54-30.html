One of my indispensable tools for research is History Hound from St. Clair Software. Although Microsoft Edge is my primary browser (see why), I use Safari, Firefox, Arc, Orion and Chrome for testing purposes. History Hound consolidates my browsing from all of those apps into a single searchable database. It indexes not only the title of websites but also the content, making it easy to find what I'm looking for with just a few keywords. It also adds bookmarks from each browser to its database, saving me the hassle of drilling through submenus to find what I'm looking for. You can try it out for 30 days for free. A single use license is $14.95.
